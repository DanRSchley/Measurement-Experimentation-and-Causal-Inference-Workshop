Suggest a Topic

This workshop is a living resource. If there is a topic, method, or concept you would like to see covered — whether from statistics, psychometrics, experimental psychology, econometrics, or any adjacent field — I would genuinely like to hear about it.

Click the button below to open a short topic request on GitHub. You do not need to provide your name or affiliation. A brief description of the topic and why it would be useful is all that is needed.

Submit a Topic Request

You will be taken to GitHub to submit an issue. A GitHub account is required. If you prefer, you can also email suggestions directly to schley@rsm.nl.
